Custom-Built Control Panels

We design and build complete electronic control panels for tower cranes, mobile cranes, boom placers, EOT cranes and passenger hoists. Whether you need a standard panel or a custom solution tailored to your crane's specific requirements, our team ensures compatibility, reliability and long equipment life.

Modernise Your Existing Panels

Old panels giving trouble? We rectify faulty panels, replace worn-out electronics, and upgrade systems to newer standards. Replace resistance boxes with modern variable speed drives for smoother starts, reduced maintenance and better motor protection.
