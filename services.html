Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Prevent costly breakdowns with regular preventive maintenance

Quarterly or Bi-annual Visits

  • Visual inspection of control panel
  • Cleaning of panel interior and exterior
  • Tightening of all terminal connections
  • Testing of limit switches and safety sensors
  • Functional testing of control circuits
  • Measurement of key electrical parameters

Load Limiter Calibration

  • Verification of hoist load limit settings
  • Calibration of load cell outputs
  • Ensuring safety margin is maintained

Component Health Check

  • Contactor wear monitoring
  • Relay response time verification
  • Soft-start or VFD performance check
  • Motor insulation resistance test

Planned Parts Replacement

  • Proactive replacement of weak components
  • Spare parts carried onsite for quick rectification
  • Minimises sudden failures during operation

Technical Troubleshooting & Fault Diagnostics

Not sure what's wrong with your crane's control panel? Our technicians use systematic diagnostic methods to pinpoint faults quickly.

Crane won't start

Power supply issues, contactor failure, safety interlock engaged

Intermittent operation

Loose connections, failing relay, moisture in panel

Load limit errors

Load cell miscalibration, wiring faults, sensor drift

Nuisance trips on DG power

Harmonic filtering needed, panel stability improvement

Joystick not responding

Wireless link lost, connector corrosion, button wear

Motor overheating

Incorrect soft-start settings, overloading, ventilation problems
