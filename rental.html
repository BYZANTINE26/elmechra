Fixed Tower Crane (FTC) Rental

A fixed tower crane is a permanent or semi-permanent structure ideal for high-rise construction, precast yards, and industrial plants with sustained lifting needs.

Typical Applications

  • High-rise building construction
  • Precast concrete and structural steel lifting
  • Bridge and infrastructure projects
  • Industrial plant construction
  • Warehouse and factory expansions

FTC Capacities Available

  • 3-ton to 10-ton capacity (standard range)
  • Custom configurations for higher capacities
  • All models with modern load monitoring

Service Included

  • Delivery to your site
  • Complete erection and commissioning
  • Electrical panel installation and testing
  • Load limiter setup and certification
  • Operator training on control system
  • Quarterly maintenance visits
  • 24-hour support for any issues
  • Dismantling and removal at project end
Minimum rental: 6 months (negotiable for projects)

Mobile Tower Crane (MTC) Rental

A mobile tower crane combines the mobility of a mobile crane with the lift capacity and speed of a tower crane. Ideal for projects that require frequent repositioning or temporary lifting at multiple sites.

Typical Applications

  • Pre-fabrication and modular construction
  • Modular tunnel segments, bridge spans
  • Rapid construction site setup and breakdown
  • Projects with changing lift points
  • Sites with limited spaces

MTC Capacities Available

  • 1.5-ton to 2.5-ton capacity (standard range)
  • Extendable boom lengths for variable heights
  • All models with smooth-start electronic control

Service Included

  • Mobilisation to your site
  • Complete setup and levelling
  • Electrical panel installation and commissioning
  • Wireless remote and joystick operation setup
  • Safety certification and load trial
  • Operator familiarisation
  • On-call support during project duration
  • Demobilisation and transport back
Minimum rental: 2 months (negotiable for projects)
