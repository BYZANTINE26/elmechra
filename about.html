About Elmechra

Customer-Focused. Proven. Innovative.

Decades of Excellence in Crane Control Systems

With decades of industry experience, Elmechra is committed to delivering innovative, reliable electrical panel solutions for cranes and heavy machinery. We put customers first, combining deep technical expertise, field-driven engineering, and a passion for continuous improvement to ensure your equipment operates at its best — every day, everywhere.

  • Tailored solutions for every crane model and application
  • Unmatched reliability and ruggedness — built for Indian conditions
  • Responsive support, fast parts supply, and expert modernization

A Vision Rooted in Real Industry Leadership

With decades of industrial experience — including leading the electrical design and development department at Action Construction Equipment — Mr. Arvind Kumar set out to build a company that raises the benchmark for crane electrical systems in India. His hands-on involvement in thousands of crane installations, upgrades, and safety-critical systems shaped the core philosophy of Elmechra:

Engineering that is practical, field-aware, and built to last.

Guided by this vision, Elmechra focuses on creating control panels and electrical solutions that offer real-world performance: simpler serviceability, higher durability, and smarter design choices that reduce downtime and extend machine life.

Our Goal

To become a trusted partner for OEMs, crane owners, and service providers by delivering electrical solutions that combine innovation with dependability — backed by transparent communication and true engineering integrity.

At Elmechra, we aim not just to support individual customers, but to contribute to the broader growth of India’s industrial and infrastructure landscape. By elevating the quality, safety, and reliability of crane electrical systems, we strive to play a meaningful role in India’s continued development and progress.
