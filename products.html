New Panel Design & Build

  • Includes all safety interlocks, load limiters and emergency stop systems
  • Tested for electrical stability on DG sets

02

Panel Rectification & Repair

  • Diagnose and repair all electronic cards, contactors, relays and power supplies
  • Replace failed components with genuine high-quality parts
  • Recalibrate limiters and safety sensors
  • Clean, tighten and inspect all terminals and wiring
  • Full functional test before return to site
  • Turnaround: 5–7 working days for most repairs
03

Complete System Upgrade

  • Modernise old or unstable panels without replacing the entire crane
  • Upgrade from resistance-box control to smooth, efficient VFDs
  • Add PLC-based control for better precision and diagnostics
  • Retrofit wireless remote operation for operator convenience
  • Integrate load monitoring and safety alerts
  • Minimal crane downtime with phased installation approach
04

DG-Suitable Panel Design

  • Special attention to power stability and harmonic filtering
  • Panels tested to operate reliably on 3-phase generator sets
  • Prevents nuisance trips and contactor chatter
  • Extends life of motors and electrical components
  • Essential for construction sites with unstable grid or backup generator power
