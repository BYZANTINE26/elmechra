Products

Field-proven electric panels for every crane and earth-moving application ‚Äî engineered for compatibility, durability, and advanced safety.

FTC (Fixed Tower Crane) Panels

Heavy-duty control panels specifically engineered for fixed tower cranes, offering seamless integration with major OEMs. Features include advanced VFD/soft-starter compatibility, high-grade safety relays, and remote diagnostics for reliable, uninterrupted operation under demanding site conditions.

MTC (Mobile Tower Crane) Panels

Robust and mobile-friendly panels designed for MTCs, ensuring reliable performance and compatibility with a wide range of mobile tower crane models. Engineered for quick deployment, shock/vibration resistance, and advanced safety interlocks for safe lifting operations.

Boom Placer Panels

Precision control panels for boom placers, supporting high-current distribution, thermal management, and intelligent safety systems. Built for easy maintenance and compatibility with all leading boom placer makes, with advanced controls for precise operation and operator safety.

Luffing Crane Panels

High-precision panels for luffing cranes with closed-loop hoist control, torque and overspeed protection, and advanced diagnostics. Designed for compatibility with modern and legacy luffing cranes, ensuring safe, smooth, and energy-efficient operations.

EOT Crane Panels

Reliable and safe panels for Electric Overhead Traveling cranes, featuring rugged enclosures, advanced protection circuits, and compatibility with a variety of EOT crane models. Incorporates programmable logic and enhanced safety for continuous industrial use.

Hydra / Telehandler Panels

Ruggedized panels built for hydras and telehandlers, utilizing dust-proof, heat-tolerant, and shock-resistant components. Fully compatible with Indian site conditions and major brands, with integrated safety controls and diagnostics for reliable field performance.

Man-Lift / AWP Panels

Advanced control panels for man-lifts and aerial work platforms, with fail-safe e-stop circuits, dual-channel safety, position sensing, and redundant control loops. Designed for compatibility, operator safety, and easy integration with all major AWP models.
